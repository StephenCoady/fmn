Diverse notifications

With different notification media, you can get updates however you want them: over email, irc, or pushed to your phone. We would send them to you in a carrier pigeon if we could.

One place

...a single place for end-users to manage notification preferences. Instead of having to tweak preferences in bodhi, koji, pkgdb, etc.. you can choose what you do and don't want to receive right here.

Ultimately, simpler

...a single place for email code to live, instead of being duplicated in every new fedoraproject.org application that we write and deploy. This will ostensibly reduce the amount of code that the infrastructure team has to maintain.
